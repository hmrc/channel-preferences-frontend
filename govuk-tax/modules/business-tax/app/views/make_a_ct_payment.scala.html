Pay your Corporation Tax

Options to pay your Corporation Tax are shown below.

It’s your responsibility to @helpers.link(Some("timeHref"), "https://www.gov.uk/prepare-file-annual-accounts-for-limited-company", "make sure payments reach HM Revenue and Customs (HMRC) on time", NewWindow) - you may be charged interest if they're late.

Your Corporation Tax payment reference number

You must use the 17-character Corporation Tax payment reference number specific to the period you're paying.

You'll find the reference on your payslip or @helpers.link(Some("ctOnlineAccountHref"), ctOnlineAccountLink.url, "accounting period", ctOnlineAccountLink.newWindow, ctOnlineAccountLink.sso).

Payments will be delayed if you don’t use the reference or get it wrong (@helpers.link(Some("ctReferenceHref"), "http://www.hmrc.gov.uk/tools/payinghmrc/ct.htm", "check your reference", NewWindow) is correctly formatted).

Direct Debit

@helpers.link(Some("btDirectDebitsHref"), ddLink.url, "Set up a Direct Debit", ddLink.newWindow, ddLink.sso).

Allow 5 working days to process a Direct Debit the first time you set one up. After that, it's 3 working days.

Payments will appear on your bank statements as "HMRC NDDS".

You can also @helpers.link(Some("btDirectDebitsHref"), ddLink.url, "change or arrange a new Direct Debit", ddLink.newWindow, ddLink.sso) online.

Online with a debit or credit card

You can @helpers.link(Some("payOnlineHref"), "https://www.santanderbillpayment.co.uk/hmrc/scripts/nrpaynow.asp", "pay online by card", NewWindow). You must have your Corporation Tax payment reference number.

Allow 3 working days for payments to reach HMRC.

You must use a card issued by a UK card issuer - not American Express or Diners Club.

There’s a 1.4% credit card fee.

Online, telephone banking, Bacs or CHAPS

Check your bank’s transaction limits and processing times.

If you make a Faster Payment it will clear the same day if the amount is within your bank’s limits.

The back of your payslip tells you which HMRC account to use. If you're not sure, use Cumbernauld.

You must use your Corporation Tax payment reference number.

If you want to pay by CHAPS, you can fill in an instruction to your bank online. CHAPS payments usually reach HMRC the same day if your bank sends them between 9am and 3pm.

Multiple CHAPS payments

To make a single payment for multiple Corporation Tax references, contact either the @helpers.link(Some("cumbernauldHref"), "http://www.hmrc.gov.uk/payinghmrc/cumbernauld.htm", "Cumbernauld", NewWindow) or @helpers.link(Some("shipleyHref"), "http://www.hmrc.gov.uk/payinghmrc/shipley.htm", "Shipley", NewWindow) accounts office.

Nothing to pay

You must @helpers.link(Some("nothingToPayHref"), "http://www.hmrc.gov.uk/payinghmrc/ct-nil.htm", "tell HMRC if you have nothing to pay", NewWindow). You will need your Corporation Tax payment reference number.

Payments for more than 1 company

If companies in a group pay under a @helpers.link(Some("groupPaymentsHref"), "http://www.hmrc.gov.uk/ct/managing/pay-repay/group-pay.htm", "Group Payment Arrangement", NewWindow) HMRC will write telling you the correct payment reference.
