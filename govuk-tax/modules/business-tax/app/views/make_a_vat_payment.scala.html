Pay your VAT

Options to pay your VAT are shown below.

It’s your responsibility to make sure payments reach HM Revenue and Customs (HMRC) on time. Use the @Link.toExternalPage(id = Some("paymentDeadlineCalculatorHref1"), url = "http://www.hmrc.gov.uk/tools/vatpaymentsdeadline/calculator.htm", value = Some("payment deadline calculator")).toHtml to make sure you pay on time - @Link.toExternalPage(id = Some("penaltyHref"), url = "https://www.gov.uk/vat-returns/surcharges-and-penalties", value = Some("you may be charged a penalty if your payment is late")).toHtml.

Direct Debit

@Link.toPortalPage( url = PortalUrlBuilder.buildPortalUrl("vatOnlineAccount"), id = Some("vatOnlineAccountHref"), value = Some("Set up a Direct Debit") ).toHtml at least 2 working days before your VAT Return and payment are due (or 3 working days, if these are due on a weekend or bank holiday). You can't pay by Direct Debit if you make payments on account.

HMRC will tell you when the Direct Debit has been set up. The @Link.toExternalPage(id = Some("paymentDeadlineCalculatorHref2"), url = "http://www.hmrc.gov.uk/tools/vatpaymentsdeadline/calculator.htm", value = Some("payment deadline calculator")).toHtml tells you when the payment leaves your account. Contact your bank to make changes to your Direct Debit.

You must pay by another method until the Direct Debit is set up.

Online with a debit or credit card

You can @Link.toExternalPage(id = Some("payOnlineHref"), url = "https://www.santanderbillpayment.co.uk/hmrc/scripts/nrpaynow.asp", value = Some("pay online with a card")).toHtml - you’ll need your VAT registration number @user.userAuthority.accounts.vat.get.vrn.vrn.

Allow 3 working days for payments to reach HMRC.
